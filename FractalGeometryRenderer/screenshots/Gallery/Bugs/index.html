Image Gallery

This page contains screenshots of some interesting visual bugs that occurred during development.

I particularly like the planet bugs 1-3 which occurred when making the planet scene. I messed something up with the noise function which gave the planet a very chaotic shape. Sierpinski cube bugs 1-3 occurred when messing around with bounding volumes, as the material of the cube was used to render the bounding volume by accident. Additionally, Mandelbulb bugs 3-5 look almost like they are an image of space, with swirling galaxies and nebulas.
